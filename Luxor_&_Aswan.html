Luxor and Aswan

About

Luxor and Aswan are the second most visited places in Egypt after Cairo. Extravagant monuments such as Karnak Temple, The Luxor Temple, Valley of Kings, Valley of Queens, Museum of Nubia and many more are the reason for this. Luxor and Aswan can be described as ones of the best places you can ever visit and spend your vacation in, especially in the winter because of theirs charming warm climate.They are characterized by their historical sites, therapeutic and business destinations which attract tourists from all over the world.
