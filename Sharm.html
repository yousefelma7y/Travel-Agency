Sharm El Sheikh

About

Sharm el Sheikh is the closest destination to Europe where you can soak up sun, dive amazing corals reefs, and enjoy the sea anytime you need a break from routine. The climate is lovely and the sun is amazing. Sharm el Sheikh boasts the widest array of fun, exciting and crazy water and extreme sports. You can easily book trips and safaris into the nearby Sinai Mountains or enjoy the amazing St Catherine Monastery. You can also enjoy the endless nights spent at local bars and beaches dancing the night away or the magic of an existing safari. Sharm is ranked among the best vacationdestinations in the world. Sharm also has it all to make your holiday better as you can also enjoy and explore Ras Mohammed National Park. You can head for your daily shopping and souvenirs in SOHO square.
